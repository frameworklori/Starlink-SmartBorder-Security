# U.S. Legal Notes – Starlink-SmartBorder-Security
© LORI Framework | compliance/Legal_Notes_US.md

Summary of key legal frameworks applicable to Starlink-SmartBorder-Security modules deployed on U.S. soil.

---

## 1. Fourth Amendment (Search & Seizure)
- Border zones have reduced expectation of privacy.
- Device scans permissible under “special needs” doctrine.

## 2. Posse Comitatus Act
- System does not activate military enforcement; Starlink modules operate under civilian command.

## 3. FAA & FCC Regulations
- Starlink uplinks operate under FCC frequency licensing.
- FAA drone integration (if any) must respect no-fly zones.

## 4. AI Regulation & AGI Risk
- AI modules are non-autonomous and designed for assistive alert only.
- All decision-making resides with human border agents.
