This document summarizes key CBP (Customs and Border Protection) operational policies relevant to the SmartBorder deployment zone.

---

## 1. Biometric Data Use
- CBP authorized to collect facial images, fingerprints, and travel history under U.S. Entry/Exit mandate.
- Facial recognition must comply with 8 CFR 235.1.

## 2. Data Retention
- Retention limited to 14 days for transient travelers; extended for flagged entities.
- Biometric info stored within DHS cloud, not accessible to commercial partners.

## 3. Exemptions
- U.S. citizens and green card holders may opt out of facial scanning at certain locations.
- Religious or medical exemptions possible but require DHS clearance.

## 4. Integration Notes
- Starlink-SmartBorder modules must integrate passively and mirror CBP’s chain-of-custody audit trail format.
