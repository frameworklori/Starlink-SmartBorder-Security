# Incident 001 – Suspected Technology Theft (Export Attempt)

---

## Trigger:
LoRa+thermal alert at pedestrian border checkpoint flagged individual carrying unknown high-density electronics.

---

## Detection:
- Device thermal signature: +12°C baseline anomaly
- Luggage flagged via gait-imbalance + edge AI alert
- Handheld scanner confirmed encrypted SSD + dual OS laptop

---

## Response Protocol:
- Secondary search authorized (no warrant needed)
- Laptop isolated + stored in Faraday pouch
- Incident logged and device detained for DHS inspection

---

## Outcome:
- Export Control Act suspected breach
- University research data confirmed on device
- Flagged for FBI tech export violation investigation
