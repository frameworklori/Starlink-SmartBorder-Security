# National Security Tech Leak Protocol
© LORI Framework

---

## Scope:
Address risk of advanced research leakage through student visas, startup visits, and outbound equipment.

---

## Indicators:
- Departure after accessing internal Git servers
- Use of dual-boot systems
- External funding flagged from Tier-2 entities

---

## Escalation:
- Notify FBI Tech Desk
- Place on “soft track” monitoring list
- Notify university’s international office

---

## Notes:
AGI-related exports = treated as high tier, regardless of nationality.
