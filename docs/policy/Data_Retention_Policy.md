## Summary:
Outlines how long data collected by SmartBorder sensors can be retained, processed, and deleted, depending on alert severity.

---

## Tiers:
| Risk Level | Retention Time | Action |
|------------|--------------------|------------------|
| Green | 72 hrs | Auto-deletion |
| Yellow | 30 days | Reviewable |
| Red | Up to 180 days | Legal hold |

---

## Privacy Safeguards:
- All biometric records are **hash-indexed** (not stored raw)
- No retention beyond period without escalation
- Retention logs are **publicly auditable**
