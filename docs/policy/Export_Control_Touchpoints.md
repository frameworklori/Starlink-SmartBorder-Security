# Export Control Touchpoints
© LORI Framework

---

## Objective:
Prevent unauthorized cross-border export of dual-use technologies by identifying risk zones and protocols.

---

## Risk Touchpoints:
- Airport terminals (cargo + USB)
- Border universities with foreign researchers
- Field sites near industry R&D zones
- Checkpoints with non-declared digital assets

---

## Flagged Technologies:
- AI chips (NVIDIA Orin, TPU)
- Research data on advanced materials
- Crypto seeds + classified comms logs

---

## Response Flow:
1. Alert → human confirm
2. Temporary hold of device
3. Warrant escalation (if needed)
