## Objective:
To respond rapidly and transparently to misuse, overreach, or unlawful activation of SmartBorder modules.

---

## Trigger Scenarios:
- Unauthorized manual override or escalation
- Misuse of biometric triggers (e.g., facial/gait/voiceprint outside policy)
- Political/ethnic targeting detected in audit trail
- Failure to disable sunset-timed module

---

## Protocol Flow:
1. **Automatic alert** to Oversight Board if breach pattern matched
2. **Freeze affected node** with timestamp + hash
3. **Public disclosure** of incident within 72 hours
4. **External review panel** convened (3 non-gov orgs)
5. **Sanctions / tech rollback** if breach confirmed

---

## Community Protections:
- Anonymous internal reporting system
- Legal immunity for whistleblowers
