## Composition:
A rotating civilian-military-academic hybrid oversight board, with fixed non-renewable terms.

---

| Seat | Stakeholder | Duration | Notes |
|------|--------------------|----------|-------|
| 1 | DHS Watch Officer | 12 mo. | Rotating |
| 2 | Civil Rights NGO | 12 mo. | E.g., ACLU |
| 3 | Academic Expert | 18 mo. | Privacy law / tech |
| 4 | Community Liaison | 12 mo. | Border region voice |
| 5 | Tech Ethics Lead | 18 mo. | Not employed by vendor |

---

## Powers:
- Approve / deny AI module upgrades
- Audit any data stream
- Recommend suspension of entire grid
- Public reporting mandate every 6 months
