# Civil Liberties Safeguards
© LORI Framework | governance/Civil_Liberties_Safeguards.md

---

## Purpose:
Ensure SmartBorder does not become a tool for mass surveillance or discrimination.

---

## Enforcement Mechanisms:
- **No face recognition** for citizens
- **No long-term biometric storage**
- **Gait/voice/thermal logs auto-purged** after 12–72 hours
- **Citizen opt-out zones** within 10mi of crossing zones
- **Oversight Board veto power** over algorithm changes

---

## Rights Included:
- Notification of flagged incidents
- Right to request log review
- Right to independent audit of their alert trail
