# Thermal IR Module
© LORI Framework | bio-scan/Thermal_IR.md

---

## Function:
Detects human body signatures based on mid-range infrared (8–14µm) thermal emission.

---

## Features:
- Humanoid body heat mapping
- Head-to-torso ratio estimation
- Crowd separation via pixel delta
- Real-time motion thermal tail tracking

## Use Mode:
- Night and low-visibility detection
- Gait + thermal fusion zone
- Animal-human differentiation

## Technical Notes:
- Frame rate: 10–30 fps
- Resolution: 320×240 or higher
- Onboard compression before uplink
