# Allowed Use Cases

✅ Life-saving detection in unmonitored deserts or ocean crossings.
✅ Alerting NGOs during high-risk refugee flow events.
✅ Cooperative monitoring during climate-induced evacuations.
✅ Cross-border epidemic early-warning via heat/movement clusters.
✅ Missing person triangulation (non-criminal, civilian-initiated).

**All use requires adherence to ZTV × LORI audit protocol.**
